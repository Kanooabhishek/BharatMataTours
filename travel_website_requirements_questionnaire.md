# Requirement-Gathering Questionnaire for a Dutch-Focused South Asia Travel Website

## Purpose of This Questionnaire

This questionnaire is designed to collect the information needed to plan, design, write, and later build a professional travel website for a company offering travel planning, guided tours, sightseeing, and curated holiday packages across India, with possible expansion into Nepal, Bhutan, and Sri Lanka.

The first version may be a dummy or prototype website, but the structure should be strong enough to later become a real business website with inquiry forms, package pages, multilingual content, SEO, booking workflows, and scalable destination management.

Suggested working assumption for phase one:

- Primary audience: Dutch travelers from the Netherlands, with possible inclusion of Dutch-speaking travelers from Belgium.
- Primary language: Dutch first, English optional.
- Primary destination focus: India first, with Nepal, Bhutan, and Sri Lanka presented as selected extensions or separate future sections.
- Initial commercial model: inquiry-based travel planning, not instant online booking.
- Pricing approach: starting prices in euros or "price on request" until supplier costs are confirmed.
- Website goal: build trust, inspire travel, explain options clearly, and generate qualified inquiries.
